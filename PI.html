PROJECT IDEA

Overview

I and my friends have a project idea of making an application for smartphones providing information about cars. We will collect the data about the cars from many brands, analyze them and show the users what they really mean. This application will not be used for buying or trading cars, it will only be used to show and analyze the cars data. Through this app, we hope people will have a clearer view of those statistics of the cars.

Motivations

There are more than 40 million of motorbikes used in Vietnam, which makes Vietnam the country that owns the second most motorbike in the world. We want Vietnam to be a more civilized country by reducing the amount of motorcycles and introducing cars to the people. Many Vietnamese people are not very familiar with the data about cars, so we want to make this application to provide them with the information.

Description

This app is only used to showing information to the users. We will show you what the data, the numbers really mean. Tools and Technologies

We, as a team, will be using JavaScript because this application will be available on both Android and IOS operation system. We will collect data from a lot of source, which means the license or permission from those car industries is extremely important.

Skills required

We have to use effectively the programming language like JavaScript and understand how it works to write the programs. We have to collaborate well with every member in the team, assign suitable work for teammates so that they can do their best. We also have to describe the data collected in the simplest way possible for everybody to understand. Last but not least, we have to have great communication skills to talk with the representatives of the brands about the ownership of the cars

Outcome

We will provide clear information for Vietnamese (and also other countries) people about cars. We hope they will have a car that is suitable for their needs and therefore reduce the amount of motorbikes used in Vietnam.
